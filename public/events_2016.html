PRUride to London Community Day

Advocating safe and responsible cycling for all, the PRUride to London 2016 closed a 5-kilometer stretch of road where cyclists enjoyed their passion in a safe environment.

To add to the festivities, games and booths were scattered along the area for all participants to enjoy.

Pictures from last year’s events can be seen here.

PRUride to London Criterium

To showcase the competitive side of cycling, PRUride to London 2016 also held a competitive criterium, where the best local amateur and professional cyclists competed against each other in a fast pace circuit around Mckinley West.
