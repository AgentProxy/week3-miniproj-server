This course is sure to test the strength and determination of our riders, as well as provide a breathtaking display of racing for the spectators.

Professional Time Trial Saturday, January 13, 2018

Time trials have been called the simplest form of bike racing - just the athlete, the bike, and the clock. However, don’t mistake simple for easy.

Unlike the professional road race, this event will feature professional cyclists working alone trying to set the best time on the 40-kilometer race course set in Subic Bay Freeport Zone. This format will push the riders to the brink and they try to gain valuable seconds.

Gran Fondo 100 Sunday, January 14, 2018

Gran Fondo is an Italian word which translates to “Big Ride”. The PRUride Gran Fondo will allow cyclists from all levels to enjoy a well organized, timed ride.

The PRUride Gran Fondo 2018 will start at Subic Bay Exhibition and Convention Center and will follow a 100-kilometer route similar to the race route to the one taken by the professional cyclists. This will take the Gran Fondo participants out of Subic Bay Freeport Zone into the picturesque roads of Bataan province, before returning to Subic Bay Freeport Zone for the finish.

Gran Fondo 50 Sunday, January 14, 2018

As part of our commitment to offer riding for all. We offer the Gran Fondo 50 for beginning cyclists.

The PRUride Gran Fondo 50 will start at Subic Bay Exhibition and Convention Center and will follow a 50-kilometer route taken by the Gran Fondo 100, but with an earlier U Turn.

This will also take the Gran Fondo 50 participants out of Subic Bay Freeport Zone into the picturesque roads of Bataan province, before returning to Subic Bay Freeport Zone for the finish.

Criterium Sunday, January 21, 2018

The PRUride Criterium will bring high octane bicycle racing in to the heart of the metro.

The PRUride Criterium will have professional and amateur cyclists competing in a 2.5-kilometer circuit in McKinley West, Taguig City. Instead of competing for a set distance, the cyclists will be competing among themselves to see who will emerge the winner after a set time. Promising a Formula 1 level of excitement with the physicality of bike racing, the PRUride Criterium 2018 is one that you should not miss, as an athlete or a spectator.

Brompton Philippine Championships Sunday, January 21, 2018

The same as Prudential RideLondon – the largest cycling festival in the world, we are bringing you the exciting Brompton Challenge along with its two events:

  1. Slowest Lap Challenge
  2. Brompton Criterium (30 mins + 2 lap race)

Slowest Lap Challenge

The slowest participant who will cross the line without stepping on the ground or returning back on the course wins the event. There will be judges following the last participant to make sure that rules are properly implemented. Track standing is allowed.

Brompton Criterium

This will be a 30-minute race plus two laps format which will follow criterium rules. All participants will start with their bikes folded at the starting line, at the sound of the horn they will run, unfold their bikes and ride. All participants must be wearing a formal top – barong, suit with tie or suit with bow-tie.

Cycling Expo

The heart of PRUride 2018, the PRUride Expo will be where athletes can pick up their race kits and watch the awarding ceremonies after each event.

There will be something for everyone, as the big bike brands in the Philippines display the latest in bicycle technology as well as have special sales on their brands.
