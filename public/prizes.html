PRIZES

Exciting prizes await you at PRUride PH 2018! From cash prizes to all-expense-paid trips to London, you will surely have something to look forward to in the two weekends of cycling festivities.

Pedal your way to London!

Six (6) all-expense-paid trips will be given away at PRUride PH 2018, including four (4) for the grand winners of the following events:

  • 160-kilometer Professional Road Race for Men (Subic)
  • 100-kilometer Professional Road Race for Women (Subic)
  • Elite Men and Women Criterium (McKinley West)

Two (2) trips will also be raffled off during the Bike Community Days in Subic and McKinely West.

Mechanics – Raffle draw for Major Prize (Trip to London)

  1. The Promotion is open to all registered participants of PRUride PH 2018, which will be held on January 11 to 14 in Subic and 21 in McKinley West, Taguig.
  2. There will be two (2) winners of the major prize, which is an all-expense-paid trip for one (1) to London, United Kingdom. One (1) winner will win in each of the two venues.
  3. To enter the raffle, participants must drop the raffle stub included in their race kit at the designated drop box immediately before the awarding ceremony programs, scheduled as follows:
    • SUBIC – 3:00 p.m. on January 14 at the Subic Bay Exhibition and Convention Center
    • TAGUIG – 7:00 p.m. on January 21 at McKinley West
  4. The event host will make a final call for entries before closing the drop box.
  5. Event officials and Pru Life UK representatives will then draw the winning numbers from the raffle drop box.
  6. Winners must be present to claim their prize. If the person holding the winning number is not present and/or doesn’t claim his/her prize in the time allocated, the organizers will draw another number.
  7. Raffle prizes are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for nor redeemed as cash or other items.
  8. Except where prohibited by law, participation in the Promotion constitutes each participant’s consent to Pru Life UK for use of their name, personal description, likeness and opinions for any promotional, marketing and publicity purposes in any media.
  9. The winner may be required to participate in publicity program which Pru Life UK may organize in connection with the Promotion and if required to do so, such winner must attend such function. Expenses and /or cost incurred by winner in attending such functions shall be for account of Pru Life UK.
  10. All winners shall agree to release, indemnify and hold Pru Life UK and partners harmless from any and all losses, damages, rights, claims and actions of any kind rising in connection with or as a result of participating in the raffle or the winner’s acceptance or use of any prize.
  11. Travel documents, taxes and other related travel-related expenses shall be shouldered by the winners.
  12. If, for any reason, the winner cannot travel to London in the time provided by Pru Life UK, his/her prize will be forfeited in favor of Pru Life UK. This prize will not be redrawn.
  13. The prize tax for all the prizes will be shouldered by Pru Life UK.
  14. The winner of the all-expense-paid trip to London must have a passport valid for at least six (6) months during the travel period or six (6) months after the redemption of prizes and must have no known restriction or prohibition to travel to any of the destination countries within the said travel period.
  15. Employees of Pru Life UK, its affiliates, suppliers, concessionaires, agents, and their relatives up to the second degree of affinity or consanguinity are disqualified from joining the promo.
